Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

The High-Value Data Network (HVDNet, "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information collection when you visit our website at highvaluedata.net (the "Site").

1. Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name, mailing address, phone number, or payment information) when you visit, browse, or use the Site. We do not require registration, user accounts, newsletters, or any forms that collect personal details.

If you contact us directly via email (e.g., at info@highvaluedata.net), we will only use your email address and the contents of your message to respond to your specific inquiry. We do not compile these email addresses into marketing lists, nor do we share them with third parties.

2. Non-Personal and Technical Data

Like most websites, we may collect or process basic non-personal technical information to ensure site performance, security, and optimization:

Derivative Data and Network Services

Our hosting servers automatically log standard web server information (such as your browser type, operating system, date/time of access, and general referrer links) for troubleshooting and security monitoring.

Additionally, we use Cloudflare for content delivery, performance optimization, and threat protection. Cloudflare may process non-personally identifiable technical telemetry data and traffic logs (such as IP addresses and request details) to optimize network delivery and identify security threats, in accordance with Cloudflare's security and privacy practices. None of this data is linked to any personal identity.

Website Analytics

Although not active at this time, we may in the future use website analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to monitor general, aggregated, and anonymous traffic patterns on the Site (such as how many visitors view a page, average session duration, etc.). If implemented in the future, these analytics will be used strictly to analyze website performance and improve our educational and data standards content, utilizing cookies to gather aggregate data. You will be able to opt out of Google Analytics at any time using browser settings or the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Local Storage (Browser Preferences)

The Site stores your user-selected visual layout preference (e.g., your chosen theme like "forest-amber", "cobalt-wave", "carbon-steel", or "cyber-coral") in your browser's local storage (localStorage). This is used solely to maintain your preferred style across pages and repeat visits. No personal data is stored in your browser's local storage.

3. Disclosure and Sharing of Data

Since we do not collect personal data from visitors, we do not sell, trade, or share user information. Technical log information is used internally only for debugging or security purposes.

4. Third-Party Websites

The Site contains links to external platforms, research organizations, and standards databases. Once you use these links to leave our Site, you are subject to the terms and privacy practices of those external sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of third-party platforms.
